# Quiz on Photosynthesis
Difficulty: Medium
Number of Questions: 5

Q1: What is the main pigment used by plants for photosynthesis?
A) Chlorophyll  
B) Hemoglobin  
C) Melanin  
D) Xanthophyll  
Correct Answer: A

Q2: Which gas is absorbed by plants during photosynthesis?
A) Oxygen  
B) Carbon dioxide  
C) Nitrogen  
D) Helium  
Correct Answer: B

Q3: Where does photosynthesis mainly occur in plant leaves?
A) Stomata  
B) Chloroplasts  
C) Roots  
D) Trunk  
Correct Answer: B

Q4: What is the byproduct of photosynthesis?
A) Oxygen  
B) Methane  
C) Sulfur  
D) Nitrogen  
Correct Answer: A

Q5: What is the primary source of energy for photosynthesis?
A) Moonlight  
B) Artificial bulbs  
C) Sunlight  
D) Fire  
Correct Answer: C
